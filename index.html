About Me

Hello! My name is Artis, and this is my first try at a digital portfolio of sorts. I’m passionate about nature and ecology, and right now I am learning how to code. On my free time I enjoy traveling, hiking and taking photos.

Current Projects

Currently I am learning how to use HTML, CSS and TypeScript for front-end oriented development. I am also getting acquainted with using Git in order to be able to develop source code in a collaborative way.

My Skills and Experience

I have studied ecology and water resource management both in the Netherlands and Latvia. I have a lot of experience developing group projects with people who come from different backgrounds and cultures and using online file sharing platforms (such as Dropbox and Google Docs) to collaborate in the development of these projects. During my studies I have learned how to analyze and process data using Microsoft Excell (and I’ve also used MATLAB for analyzing large volumes of data). I know how to use MS Word to write concise and readable reports. I have also learned how to use MS PowerPoint to visually present data and information in way that is a clear and easy to understand.

And I also have a basic understanding of HTML, CSS and JavaScript, and how to apply them to create simple web-pages.
